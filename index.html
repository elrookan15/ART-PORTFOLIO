About the Artist

"I am not a product of an art school; I am a product of the process."

My work is born from a lack of formal training, which I view not as a limitation, but as a freedom. Without the weight of traditional 'rules,' I am able to approach the canvas with a singular goal: to tell a story.

Every layer of paint, every scraped texture, and every bold contrast is a chapter in a narrative that begins with my hand but is completed by the viewer's eye. I don't aim to provide answers or a fixed map of what a painting 'means.' Instead, I create the architecture of a story—the mood, the tension, and the energy—and leave the final interpretation to you.

What you see in the chaos is your own. My art is simply the invitation to look.
